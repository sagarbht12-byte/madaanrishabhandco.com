Frequently Asked Questions

Typically 10–15 working days for a Private Limited Company, depending on MCA processing times and document completeness. With DSC and DIN in place, we often complete filings within 7 days.
GSTR-1 is due by the 11th of the following month (monthly filers). GSTR-3B is due by the 20th. Annual return GSTR-9 is due by 31st December following the financial year end. Quarterly filers under QRMP have different due dates.
Software helps with data entry, but a CA ensures accuracy, statutory compliance, tax optimization, and audit readiness. Most businesses benefit significantly from professional oversight even when using software like Tally or Zoho.
For individuals: Form 16, bank statements, investment proofs, rental income docs. For businesses: audited financials, GST returns, TDS certificates, and details of all income sources. We help you compile the complete checklist.
An LLP offers flexible management, lower compliance burden, and no minimum capital requirement, but can't raise equity funding. A Pvt Ltd allows equity investment, ESOP issuance, and unlimited partners, with more regulatory requirements. We help you choose the right structure.
